「The Kichael Mo Project」

In six weeks, our team of four was to build a fully autonomous robot.

The Course

ENPH 253 is a course taken by 2nd-year Engineering Physics student at UBC in teams of four that embark on the design and construction of autonomous robots to compete in a publicly attended competition.

The Competition

The theme for 2018 was “Ewok Rescue: A Star Warz Story”, the task is to navigate a miniaturized stronghold, retrieve five toy dolls and bring them back to the starting point under two minutes.

Robot Equipment

Our robot is powered by three LiPo batteries and equipped with three DC motors, five servo motors, two microcontrollers, as well as various sensors.

Robot Capabilities

It is capable of tape-following, target pickups, edge-sensing, gap-crossing, IR frequency detection, and platform-lifting.
